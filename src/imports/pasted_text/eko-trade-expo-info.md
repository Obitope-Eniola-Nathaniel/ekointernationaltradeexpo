ABOUT THE EKO INTERNATIONAL TRADE EXPO

The Eko International Trade Expo is a premier multi-sectoral trade exhibition designed to promote commerce, innovation, and international partnerships within Nigeria and across global markets. Inspired by the vibrant commercial spirit and rich cultural heritage of Lagos fondly known as “Eko” the Expo serves as a dynamic platform where businesses, investors, entrepreneurs, and institutions converge to explore new opportunities and expand market frontiers.

Eko is more than a city; it is a symbol of enterprise, resilience, diversity, and cultural pride. The Eko International Trade Expo reflects this identity by bringing together industries, ideas, and people from different parts of the world into one thriving marketplace of possibilities.

Organised by Momentum Trading Enterprises, the Expo aims to showcase the strength of Nigerian enterprise while connecting local businesses with international markets, investors, and strategic partners.

THE EXPO EXPERIENCE

The Eko International Trade Expo is designed as a vibrant business and cultural marketplace where innovation meets opportunity. The event features a wide range of sectors including manufacturing, agriculture, technology, beauty and cosmetics, fashion, services, and creative industries.

Participants experience more than a conventional trade exhibition. The Expo creates an environment that celebrates business, creativity, and the cultural energy that defines Lagos.

Key highlights include:

1.	International and local exhibitions
2.	Business-to-business (B2B) networking sessions
3.	Investment and trade promotion opportunities
4.	Product launches and brand showcases
5.	Government and diplomatic engagement platforms
6.	SME development and market access opportunities
7.	Cultural showcases reflecting the spirit of Eko

OBJECTIVES OF THE EXPO

The Eko International Trade Expo is designed to achieve the following:

1.	Promote international trade and investment opportunities in Nigeria
2.	Provide a strategic platform for SMEs to access markets and partnerships
3.	Facilitate business networking and collaboration between local and international companies
4.	Encourage innovation and product development across industries
5.	Strengthen economic and commercial ties between Nigeria and global markets
6.	Showcase the entrepreneurial spirit and cultural richness of Lagos

TARGET PARTICIPANTS

The Expo attracts a diverse group of stakeholders including:

1.	Manufacturers and exporters
2.	Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
3.	International companies seeking African market entry
4.	Government agencies and trade promotion organisations
5.	Investors and venture capital firms
6.	Diplomatic missions and international trade bodies
7.	Service providers and industry professionals

ORGANISER

The Eko International Trade Expo is organised by Momentum Trading Enterprises, a professional trade promotion and exhibition management company committed to creating world-class platforms that support business growth and international collaboration.

With strong industry experience and deep institutional knowledge in trade fair management, Momentum Trading Enterprises delivers professionally managed exhibitions that connect businesses, promote investment, and drive economic development.

LEADERSHIP

The Expo is led by Henry Anwansedo, Founder and Managing Director of Momentum Trading Enterprises. With over a decade of practical experience in trade fair promotion and exhibition coordination, he brings extensive expertise in exhibitor engagement, trade promotion, sponsorship development, and stakeholder management.

Henry previously spent eight years with the Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry (LCCI), contributing significantly to the organisation of the Lagos International Trade Fair. He also served with the Lagos International Trade Fair Complex Management Board under the Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade and Investment, gaining valuable experience in venue operations and event logistics.

He is also an alumnus of the 2019 JICA Training Programme on Financial Access for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) Promotion in Japan, which further strengthened his capacity in SME development and trade facilitation.

Driven by a strong passion for economic development and enterprise growth, Henry leads the Eko International Trade Expo with a vision to build a globally recognised trade platform that promotes Nigerian businesses while fostering international partnerships.

A PLATFORM INSPIRED BY EKO

Eko has long been the commercial heartbeat of Nigeria a city where culture, creativity, and commerce thrive side by side. The Eko International Trade Expo embraces this identity by creating a marketplace that reflects the energy, diversity, and entrepreneurial spirit of Lagos.

From local entrepreneurs to international investors, the Expo provides a space where ideas flourish, partnerships are formed, and businesses grow.
